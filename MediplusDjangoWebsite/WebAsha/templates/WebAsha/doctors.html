Meet Our Specialists

  • Dr. Aditi Sharma - Cardiologist

    Dr. Aditi Sharma has over 15 years of experience in cardiology. She specializes in interventional cardiology and has performed over 500 successful angioplasties. Dr. Sharma is known for her patient-centric approach and dedication to heart health.

  • Dr. Rajesh Kapoor - Neurologist

    Dr. Rajesh Kapoor is a renowned neurologist with 20 years of experience. His expertise includes treating stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases. He has published numerous papers in prestigious journals and is actively involved in neurological research.

  • Dr. Priya Mehta - Orthopedic Surgeon

    Dr. Priya Mehta specializes in joint replacement surgeries and sports injuries. She has been practicing for 12 years and has a high success rate in knee and hip replacements. Dr. Mehta is committed to providing personalized care and ensuring quick recovery for her patients.

  • Dr. Sanjay Gupta - Gastroenterologist

    Dr. Sanjay Gupta has expertise in digestive system disorders, including IBS, Crohn's disease, and liver diseases. He has 18 years of clinical experience. Dr. Gupta is dedicated to advancing gastrointestinal health through patient education and innovative treatments.

  • Dr. Neha Verma - Pediatrician

    Dr. Neha Verma has 10 years of experience in pediatrics. She focuses on child development, preventive healthcare, and the treatment of acute and chronic illnesses in children. Dr. Verma is known for her compassionate care and commitment to the well-being of her young patients.
