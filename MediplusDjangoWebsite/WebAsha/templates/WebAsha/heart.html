Common Heart Conditions

  • Coronary Artery Disease (CAD): A condition where the heart's arteries are narrowed or blocked.
  • Heart Failure: A condition where the heart does not pump blood effectively.
  • Arrhythmias: Abnormal heart rhythms that can affect heart function.

Diagnostic Tests

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG): Measures the electrical activity of the heart.
  • Chest X-ray: Provides images of the heart and lungs.
  • Stress Test: Assesses heart function during physical activity.

Treatment Options

  • Medications: To manage symptoms, reduce blood pressure, or prevent blood clots.
  • Procedures: Such as angioplasty, stent placement, or heart valve repair.
  • Surgery: Including coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG) or heart transplants.

Prevention Tips

  • Healthy Lifestyle: Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and avoiding smoking.
  • Regular Monitoring: Keeping track of blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
  • Manage Stress: Techniques to reduce stress and improve heart health.
