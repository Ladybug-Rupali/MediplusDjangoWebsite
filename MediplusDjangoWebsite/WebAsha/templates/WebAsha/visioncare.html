Vision Care

Taking care of your vision is essential for maintaining eye health and preventing visual impairments. Regular eye exams and a healthy lifestyle can support good vision.

Common Vision Problems

  • Myopia: Nearsightedness where distant objects appear blurry.
  • Hyperopia: Farsightedness where close objects are blurry.
  • Astigmatism: An irregular cornea shape causing blurred vision.
  • Cataracts: Clouding of the eye's lens leading to decreased vision.

Care and Treatment

To care for your vision, consider:

  • Eyeglasses or Contact Lenses: For correcting refractive errors.
  • Medications: To treat eye infections or dryness.
  • Laser Surgery: To correct vision issues like myopia or astigmatism.
  • Surgery: For conditions like cataracts or retinal problems.

Vision Preservation

To preserve vision:

  • Have regular eye exams.
  • Protect your eyes from UV light.
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in eye-friendly nutrients.
  • Take breaks from screens to reduce eye strain.
