Consulting Services

Mediplus Hospital offers expert consulting services to assist with complex medical conditions. Our experienced consultants are available to provide in-depth evaluations, second opinions, and specialized advice tailored to your healthcare needs.

Specialist Consultations

We offer consultations with a range of specialists, including cardiologists, orthopedic surgeons, and more. Our goal is to provide you with comprehensive insights and treatment options based on your individual health requirements.

How to Schedule a Consultation

To schedule a consultation, please contact our office directly or use our online appointment system. Our staff will assist you in arranging a convenient time for your consultation with one of our experts.

Consultation Fees

Consultation fees vary depending on the specialist and the complexity of the case. For detailed information about fees, please contact our billing department.
