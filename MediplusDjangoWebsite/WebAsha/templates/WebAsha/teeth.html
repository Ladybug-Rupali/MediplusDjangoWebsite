Common Dental Issues

  • Cavities: Decay in teeth caused by plaque buildup.
  • Gum Disease: Inflammation or infection of the gums.
  • Tooth Sensitivity: Pain or discomfort in teeth caused by various factors.

Diagnostic Procedures

  • Dental X-rays: Images of the teeth and jaw to identify problems.
  • Oral Exam: A comprehensive examination to check for issues.
  • Periodontal Probing: Measures gum health and the depth of gum pockets.

Treatment Options

  • Fillings: To restore cavities and damaged teeth.
  • Cleanings: Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar.
  • Root Canals: To treat infection inside a tooth.

Oral Health Tips

  • Brush and Floss Daily: To maintain good oral hygiene.
  • Regular Dental Visits: For check-ups and cleanings.
  • Healthy Diet: Limit sugary foods and drinks that can lead to dental issues.
