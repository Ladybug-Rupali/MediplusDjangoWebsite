Vision treatments address issues related to eyesight and eye health. Hereâ€™s a detailed breakdown:

Common Eye Conditions

  • Myopia (Nearsightedness): Difficulty seeing distant objects clearly.
  • Hyperopia (Farsightedness): Difficulty seeing close objects clearly.
  • Cataracts: Clouding of the eye's lens, affecting vision.

Diagnostic Procedures

  • Visual Acuity Test: Measures the sharpness of your vision.
  • Eye Pressure Test: Assesses intraocular pressure to check for glaucoma.
  • Retinal Exam: Examines the back of the eye for signs of damage or disease.

Treatment Options

  • Corrective Lenses: Glasses or contact lenses to correct vision problems.
  • Medications: To treat eye infections or inflammatory conditions.
  • Surgery: Procedures like LASIK or cataract surgery to improve vision.

Eye Health Tips

  • Regular Eye Exams: To detect and address issues early.
  • Protect Your Eyes: Use sunglasses to shield from UV rays.
  • Healthy Diet: Foods rich in vitamins and minerals that support eye health.
