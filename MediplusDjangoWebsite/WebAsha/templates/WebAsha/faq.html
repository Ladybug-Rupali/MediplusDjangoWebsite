Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • What are your hospital hours? We are open 24/7 for emergencies. For non-emergency services, please check our operating hours on our website.
  • How can I make an appointment? You can schedule an appointment by calling our office or using our online booking system.

Insurance and Billing

  • Do you accept insurance? Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more details.
  • What forms of payment do you accept? We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, cash, and checks.

Patient Care

  • What should I bring to my appointment? Please bring your insurance card, identification, and any relevant medical records.
  • Can I visit a patient? Yes, visitors are allowed during designated visiting hours. Please adhere to our visiting policies.
