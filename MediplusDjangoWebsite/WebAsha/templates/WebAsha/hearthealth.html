HEART HEALTH TIPS

Maintaining heart health is essential for overall well-being. Adopting heart-healthy habits can significantly reduce the risk of cardiovascular diseases and enhance quality of life.

Healthy Eating

  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Reduce intake of saturated and trans fats.
  • Limit sodium and added sugars.
  • Incorporate lean proteins like fish and poultry.

Regular Exercise

Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week. Activities like brisk walking, jogging, and swimming are excellent for heart health.

Avoid Smoking

Smoking is a major risk factor for heart disease. Quitting smoking improves heart health and reduces the risk of heart attacks and strokes.

Regular Check-ups

Regular health check-ups help monitor heart health and detect any potential issues early. Blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and other heart-related parameters should be regularly assessed.

Stress Management

Chronic stress can negatively impact heart health. Techniques such as mindfulness, meditation, and relaxation exercises can help manage stress effectively.
